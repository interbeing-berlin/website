**What**<br>
Community rituals to reconnect with Life.

**How**<br>
Gathering each week to meditate, sing, move, and dialogue together, in a place of reverence, inquiry, intention and community.

**Where**<br>
Genezareth Kirche in Schillerkiez, Berlin (and other places around Berlin, Europe and the world).

**When**<br>
Services on Sundays, 13:00 – 14:30. Chanting evening 1st Sunday of the month. Being Lab every Tuesday evening. Morning meditations 3 days/week.

**Why**<br>
As humans, we are sustained and animated by connection with ourselves, each other, and the wider wholes we are part of; by experiences of the sacred, the numinous, or the transcendent; and by community. In these times we need the sustenance of connection. With it, our capacity to care for ourselves, for each other, and for the life around us is empowered, and a doorway opens to a more beautiful world.
